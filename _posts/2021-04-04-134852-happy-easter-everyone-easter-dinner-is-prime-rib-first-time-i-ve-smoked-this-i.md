Happy Easter everyone. Easter dinner is Prime Rib, first time I’ve smoked this... I started at 530 let it warm up and put it in the smoker at 8..low heat (200-250) until the internal temp was around 115 then flipped it until the internal temp was about 125 then wrapped it in foil until it got to 130 then pulled it out and let it set before slicing it up. For a first time I think it turned out well and was very tasty.
