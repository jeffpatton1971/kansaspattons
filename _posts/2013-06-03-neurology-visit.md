We made a trip to visit with the Neurologist today. Nathan’s oncologist wanted us to meet with a neurologist to get a baseline of Nathan’s neuropathy. In some of the pictures you can see that his right eye is droopy, when he was in the hospital last it was really bad. In addition to that he also is experiencing some pain, in his arms, feet and his head. I think the worse part about all of this is, this is Nathan’s new normal, lately he doesn’t know what it’s like to not hurt somewhere or visit a doctor at least once a week. We’ll all be very glad when this is all behind us.
