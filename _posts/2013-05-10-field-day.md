Today was field day at Natalie’s school. After Nathan’s chemo we took him over to Natalie’s school for field day. It started out rather cool, and honestly it didn’t really get nice until almost 3, but the kids all had fun. Natalie is very ready for this school year to be over I think. They had several events, running track, obstacle course, long jumps, tug of war, sack races, and so on. We missed the first few of them, but got to see Natalie run, she is pretty darn quick! Maybe she will be interested in track when she gets older!
